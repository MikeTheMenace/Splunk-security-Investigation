## RECCOMENDATIONS
Phishing is the most common way threat actors gain initial access into the enterprise environment and for this I reccomend
a) User Training on emails.
b) Email filtering and attachment scanning
c)URL Sandboxing allowing URLs to be opened in an isolated environment
